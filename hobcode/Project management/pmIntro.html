Course Overview :

In today's competitive business landscape, product managers play a pivotal role in shaping a company's success. This course is designed to equip you with a holistic understanding of the product management process. It's not just about developing products; it's about envisioning their success, crafting effective business strategies, and tackling technical challenges head-on.

Key Learning points:
